In this project, a Python script was created to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. To accomplish this, a simple Python library, the OpenWeatherMap API, and a little common sense was used to create a representative model of weather across world cities. A series of scatter plots were created to showcase the following relationships:

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
    •
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
    •
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
    •
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
